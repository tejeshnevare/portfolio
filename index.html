My Services

GeM Portal Services

GeM (Government e-Marketplace) Portal is a platform for procurement of goods and services by government organizations. I offer freelancing services to assist businesses in registering, managing their profiles, and navigating the GeM Portal effectively to maximize opportunities and compliance.

E-Tendering

E-Tendering involves the electronic submission of tenders and bids for various projects and services. I offer freelancing services to assist businesses in navigating the e-tendering process, ensuring compliance, and maximizing their chances of success in securing contracts.

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing involves promoting products or services through digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and websites. I offer freelancing services to help businesses enhance their online presence, drive traffic, and achieve their marketing goals effectively.


SEO

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) involves optimizing websites to rank higher on search engine results pages (SERPs), driving organic traffic and improving visibility. I offer freelancing services to help businesses enhance their online presence, implement effective SEO strategies, and achieve sustainable growth.

SMM

SMM (Social Media Marketing) involves leveraging social media platforms to promote products, services, or brands. I offer freelancing services to help businesses create effective social media strategies, manage campaigns, and engage with their audience to drive growth and brand awareness.
